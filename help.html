Help & Support

Welcome to MisnDoow Help Center. Here you can understand the website’s features and find solutions to common problems.

🔍 How to use Search?

Type a word in the search box and press Enter, or click the Send button.

📌 How do suggestions work?

As you type, suggestions will automatically appear.

📄 How are results displayed?

The system fetches live summaries from Wikipedia and displays them.

⚠️ Having a problem?

Check your internet connection or refresh the page. If the issue persists, contact the developer.
